Lazy Lunker Lodge Activities

Lazy Lunker Lodge offers many different activities for your entertainment and enjoyment.

To view some of the options, click the links below:

  • Fishing
  • Hiking
  • Canoeing

Fishing

Both seasoned and new anglers love fishing on Lake WannaBeHere. A wide variety of fish species offer exciting opportunities for anglers of all types and abilities. You can fish off our docks, bring your own boat, or can rent fishing boats for $20 a day.

Hiking

The forests surrounding Lazy Lunker Lodge are filled with wildlife and waiting to be explored. We offer 25km of trails for your hiking pleasure. You can go out on your own, or we will provide a tour guide who is an expert of the area with many stories to share. Keep an eye out for Milly Moose, a majestic creature who likes to play hide and seek with our guests.

Canoeing

If you've ever wanted to learn to paddle a canoe, Lazy Lunker Lodge offers you the chance with daily lesson and canoe rental packages. Enjoy the serenity of our peaceful lake as you glide through the calm waters of Lake WannaBeHere.
